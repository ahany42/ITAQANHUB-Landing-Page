Our Partners

At ITQAN HUB, We prioritize partnering with organizations upholding the highest standards of quality and reliability. These partnerships guarantee the development of premium products, fostering a mutually beneficial environment that collectively expands our capabilities and market reach. Our diverse network of partners contributes valuable expertise and creative solutions, enabling us to tackle complex challenges with agility and innovation.

ITQAN HUB has been accredited as an authorized training center by FORTINET, spanning Egypt, Libya, and Sudan. This partnership is a testament to our unwavering commitment to providing our customers with exceptional services and offerings, marking a new era of growth and innovation. In addition, it facilitates FORTINET’s expansion into these three significantly large markets.

The Fortinet NSE certification

The Fortinet Network Security Expert (NSE) Certification Program has been meticulously designed and extensively researched over an extended period to effectively address the evolving threat landscape and job market demands. Each certification level within the program evaluates distinct levels of cybersecurity knowledge, ranging from foundation to architect. Our utmost objective is to fulfill both current and future demands, ensuring that the NSE Certification Program consistently retains its position as a leader in network security proficiency.

The certification program provided by ITQAN HUB encompasses a wide array of courses across diverse tracks. These courses are delivered in various formats, providing participants with the flexibility to select the most suitable option for their learning style. Furthermore, the program integrates hands-on exercises conducted through robust labs, effectively demonstrating intricate security concepts. By offering such comprehensive training opportunities, ITQAN HUB ensures that candidates acquire a thorough understanding and expertise in their chosen field of study.
